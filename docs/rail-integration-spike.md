# Rail Integration Spike

## Objective
Establish a viable provider route for live UK rail return-fare search, booking, purchase, and ticket fulfilment retrieval.

## Candidate Providers

### 1) SilverRail
- **Why investigate**: Rail retail/distribution platform with existing commerce capabilities.
- **Access route to confirm**: Partner onboarding, sandbox/certification path, UK rail product availability.
- **Status**: Planned outreach.

### 2) Trainline Partner Route
- **Why investigate**: Proven high-scale UK ticket retailing and digital fulfilment.
- **Access route to confirm**: API/affiliate/embedded booking capability for third-party products.
- **Status**: Planned outreach.

### 3) RDG / RSP pathway
- **Why investigate**: Industry route for accredited retailing, settlement and ticket issuing.
- **Access route to confirm**: Accreditation requirements for prototype and production.
- **Status**: Planned outreach.

## Integration Questions to Resolve
- Is sandbox or controlled-live testing available?
- Can UK return fares be searched and purchased?
- Which return products are available (e.g. Anytime Day Return)?
- What fulfilment artefacts are returned (barcode/PDF/reference)?
- Is fare reservation supported before final purchase?
- Who is merchant of record and how does settlement work?
- What production/commercial constraints apply?
